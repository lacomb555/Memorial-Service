In Loving Memory of Bill Acomb

Beloved Father, Grandfather, Brother, and Friend

It is with deep love and profound sorrow that we announce the passing of William Edward Acomb, Jr., a remarkable man whose life was rich with love, laughter, and curiosity.

Born to Bill Sr. and Fritzi, and raised alongside his adored older sisters Nancy and Joyce, Bill brought joy and just the right dose of mischief to everyone lucky enough to know him.

A respected general contractor and business owner, Bill poured his heart into his work, treating colleagues and clients like family. His projects were built to last—just like his most cherished relationships.

Outside of work, Bill’s hands were rarely still. He found joy in restoring his beloved 1969 Camaro with his son, building wood projects in the barn with his daughter, and unwinding at Lake Chelan.

Bill was a devoted father and grandfather. To Lisa, Jeff, Enzo, and Nico, he was a valued life coach, project partner, and provider of unwavering support. Oh yes, and funny as hell!

He will be missed beyond measure and remembered forever, in the scent of sawdust, the rumble of a V8 engine, and the still waters of Lake Chelan at sunset.

Rest easy, Dad. We’ll carry your tools from here.

— Lisa & Jeff
