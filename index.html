The Final Lap

"Bill’s life was a masterclass in craftsmanship. Whether he was blueprinting a dream, fine-tuning his beloved 1969 Camaro, or navigating the open roads toward Lake Chelan, he did it all with precision, a sly smile, and a generous heart."

He didn’t just drive through life; he embraced every gear. We invite you to join us at NW Differential to share a story, the rev of an engine, and a final toast to a man who built things to last.
